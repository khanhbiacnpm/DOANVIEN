DotNetNuke Child Skin

Introduction

Congratulations! You have created your own DotNetNuke Skin.  Please take a few minutes to read this document, which will help you install and configure your new skin.

* Important *

Due to limitations in the templating capabilities of Visual Studio, you will need to manually rename a couple of folders before you continue.

Rename /Portals/0/Containers/SkinName to /Portals/0/Containers/Child

Rename /Portals/0/Skins/SkinName to /Portals/0/Skins/Child

This can be accomplished in your development environment by right-clicking the folder name in the Solutions Explorer and selecting Rename from the menu.
